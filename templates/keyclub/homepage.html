What is the Key Club App/automation program?

It is a program I wrote to automate logging Key Club volunteer hours.
Normally, a Key Club officer would have to log every hour for every event manually,
and input all the hours from the sign up sheets to the hours spreadsheet by hand,
but now they can use their Google account to log into my Key Club App,
and it will log the hours for them automatically, once they provide a link to a sign up sheet.
